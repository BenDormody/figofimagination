About

My name is Ben, and this is my personal poetry page to share with people.

I'm inspired by the works of Maya Angelou, John Berryman, Pablo Neruda, and many other incredible poets who have shaped the way I see and express the world through words.

I hope people enjoy reading my poetry, and I encourage you to reach out to me on Instagram with any thoughts, advice, or comments. I would love to hear from you and connect (Even if just to get some good movie/music/book/poetry recommendations).
